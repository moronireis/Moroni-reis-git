## 1. System Overview

The Raiz/Nutella system is a content production framework where ALL short-form content (Nutella) is derived from long-form content (Raiz). This ensures message consistency, maximizes production efficiency, and creates a content ecosystem where each piece reinforces the others.

### Flow:
```
Topic Selection (5 techniques)
  → Raiz Production (30min+ video)
    → Raiz Distribution (paid traffic)
      → Nutella Derivation (3-5 short pieces per Raiz)
        → Nutella Distribution (organic + paid)
          → Audience Growth → More Raiz Topics
```

## 2. Raiz Content Production

### Definition:
- Minimum 30 minutes of dense, deep-dive content
- The definitive piece on that topic
- Establishes you as the go-to expert
- Creates retargeting audiences (video viewers)

### 5 Topic Generation Techniques:

#### Technique 1: 3 Areas of the Roma
Break the destination into 3 sub-areas and create Raiz for each.

Example (Roma: "Faturar com IA"):
- Area 1: Revenue attribution with AI
- Area 2: Cost reduction through automation
- Area 3: Team augmentation with AI agents

#### Technique 2: Dores e Aspirações
Map the avatar's pains and desires. One Raiz per major pain/desire.

Example:
- Pain: "Invested R$500K in AI with zero ROI" → Raiz: "Why 87% of AI projects fail (and what to do instead)"
- Desire: "Be the CEO who cracked the AI code" → Raiz: "How to build an AI revenue strategy in 90 days"

#### Technique 3: Mitos (Myth Busting)
Identify and destroy myths in the niche.

Example:
- Myth: "AI will replace all jobs" → Raiz: "AI doesn't replace people. It replaces PROCESSES."
- Myth: "You need a huge tech team for AI" → Raiz: "How solo founders are implementing AI with 3-person teams"

#### Technique 4: Atalhos e Obstáculos
Share shortcuts that save time and obstacles that waste it.

Example:
- Shortcut: "The 3 things to validate before any AI project" → saves months of wasted work
- Obstacle: "The Prototype Graveyard — why beautiful POCs never generate revenue"

#### Technique 5: Estudos de Caso
Deep-dive into real results with specific numbers.

Example:
- "How Company X went from R$0 to R$2.3M in AI-attributed revenue in 6 months"
- "The exact 5-step process we used to save R$800K/year in operational costs"

### Raiz Script Structure:
1. **Hook** (first 30 seconds) — Why this matters NOW
2. **Context** — Set the stage, establish the problem
3. **Connection to Roma** — "This connects to [destination] because..."
4. **Main Content** (20-25 min) — The deep-dive teaching
5. **Proof** — Cases, data, testimonials
6. **Bridge to Offer** (optional) — "If you want help implementing this..."
7. **CTA** — Subscribe, follow, comment, or event registration

## 3. Nutella Content Derivation

### Definition:
- Short-form content (15s-60s for Reels/Shorts, 1-3min for extended)
- ALWAYS derived from an existing Raiz — never original
- Purpose: reach new audiences, drive to Raiz

### Derivation Methods:

| Method | How | Example |
|--------|-----|---------|
| **Key Moment Clip** | Extract the most powerful 30-60 seconds | The "aha moment" from a 40-min Raiz |
| **Thesis Statement** | Take the core argument in 15-30 seconds | "87% of AI projects fail because..." |
| **Before/After** | Show the transformation in short format | "Before AI strategy: chaos. After: R$2.3M" |
| **Reaction/Commentary** | React to the Raiz topic in short format | Quick take on the myth busted |
| **Carousel Summary** | Turn Raiz into 10-slide carousel | Key frameworks visualized |
| **Quote Card** | Pull a powerful quote, design it | Static image for feed posts |

### Production Ratio:
- Every Raiz should generate 3-5 Nutella pieces
- Weekly: 1-2 Raiz → 5-10 Nutella
- Monthly: 4-8 Raiz → 20-40 Nutella pieces

## 4. Distribution Strategy

### Raiz Distribution:
1. Publish on YouTube (primary) + podcast platforms
2. Allocate minimum paid budget for distribution
3. Target: custom audiences + lookalikes
4. Objective: video views (to build retargeting audiences)
5. Track: view duration, completion rate, engagement

### Nutella Distribution:
1. Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts (simultaneous)
2. Organic posting + boosted top performers
3. Cross-promote: "Full video on YouTube" → drive to Raiz
4. Track: reach, saves, shares, profile visits

### Paid Distribution (4-Step Process):
1. **Budget**: Define weekly/monthly distribution budget
2. **Publish**: Post the Raiz on all platforms
3. **Boost**: Invest in distribution immediately (don't wait for organic)
4. **Collect**: Track data, build retargeting audiences from viewers

## 6. Conteúdo Raiz for Reels (Insider Method)

### Reels Structure from Insider:
The Insider program provides specific Reels frameworks:
1. **Problem → Solution** (15-30s)
2. **Myth Buster** (15-30s)
3. **Quick Tip** (15-30s)
4. **Behind the Scenes** (15-60s)
5. **Transformation Story** (30-60s)

### Reels Engagement Rules:
- Hook in first 2 seconds (pattern interrupt or bold statement)
- Text overlays for silent viewing
- CTA in last 3 seconds
- Trending audio when appropriate (but not forced)
- Face-to-camera builds more trust than B-roll alone

## 7. Content and Launch Connection

### Método LEOA Priority:
1. **L — Lançamento**: Get launch mechanics right first
2. **E — Entrega**: Deliver excellent product experience
3. **O — Oferta**: Optimize offer based on feedback
4. **A — Audiência**: Scale audience building

Content serves the launch, not the other way around. Don't build audience indefinitely without launching.

### Objection Weakening Through Content:
- Map the 5 universal objections + 5 specific ones
- Create content that addresses each BEFORE the pitch
- By launch day, 80% of objections should be already weakened
- Track which objections persist → create more content targeting them
