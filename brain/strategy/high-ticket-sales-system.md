## 1. FHT Masterplan Overview

The Formula High Ticket is a complete system for selling high-ticket products (R$3K-R$50K+) through 1-on-1 sessions, live immersions, and structured funnels.

### Core Products in a HT Business:

| Product Type | Price Range | Delivery | Scale |
|---|---|---|---|
| **DFY (Done For You)** | R$15K-50K+ | You do it for them | Low scale, high profit |
| **DWY (Done With You)** | R$5K-15K | Mentorship/coaching | Medium scale |
| **DIY (Do It Yourself)** | R$500-5K | Course/program | High scale, lower ticket |

### Value Ladder (Bottom to Top):
1. Free content → build trust
2. Low-ticket (R$47-497) → prove value, acquire customers
3. Mid-ticket (R$500-3K) → group programs, courses
4. High-ticket (R$3K-15K) → mentorship, DWY
5. Ultra-ticket (R$15K-50K+) → DFY, done for you services

## 2. Sessão 1a1 (1-on-1 Sales Session)

### Pre-Session:
1. **Lead research**: Analyze application form, social media, business context
2. **Identify**: Pain points, dreams, hidden objections, decision-making style
3. **Prepare**: Customized talking points based on lead profile

### Session Script (60-90 min):

#### Phase 1 — Rapport & Context (10 min)
- Open with genuine interest in their business
- "Tell me about your business and where you are right now"
- Active listening, note key phrases for later callback

#### Phase 2 — Diagnosis (20 min)
- "What's your biggest challenge right now?"
- "If you could change one thing, what would it be?"
- "What have you already tried?"
- "What would it mean for you to solve this in the next 90 days?"
- Map their DSA (Dores, Sonhos, Aspirações)

#### Phase 3 — Vision of Solution (15 min)
- Paint the Roma — the specific destination
- "Here's what I see as possible for you..."
- Connect THEIR words to YOUR method
- Present the method as the bridge from where they are to where they want to be

#### Phase 4 — Offer (10 min)
- Present the complete offer (name, deliverables, bonuses, guarantee)
- Stack the value before revealing price
- "The investment for this is [price]"
- Silence — let them process

#### Phase 5 — Objection Handling (15 min)
- Use the 48 Laws + DISC awareness
- Never argue — align and redirect
- "I totally understand. Let me ask you this..."
- The Choice Paradox: maximum 2 options when closing

#### Phase 6 — Close (5 min)
- "So what would you like to do?"
- If yes: immediate next steps, payment link
- If no/maybe: follow-up sequence activated

### Post-Session:
- Log in tracking spreadsheet: Date / Closer / Wins / Errors / Objections / Bought?
- Identify patterns for improvement
- Follow-up sequence: Day 1, 3, 7, 14

## 3. Lead Generation for Sessions

### 5 Funnel Types for HT:

#### 3.1 Free Bait Funnel (Isca Gratuita)
- Free content/webinar → application form → qualified leads → session
- Highest volume, lowest qualification
- Best for: building pipeline when starting

#### 3.2 Paid Bait Funnel (Isca Paga)
- Low-ticket product (R$47-497) → buyer list → session invitation
- Lower volume, higher qualification (they already paid)
- Best for: filtering serious prospects

#### 3.3 Application Funnel (Funil de Aplicação)
- Content → application page → qualifying questions → session
- Medium volume, high qualification
- Best for: premium positioning

#### 3.4 Social Selling Funnel
- Organic content → DM conversations → session booking
- Lowest cost, highest personal effort
- Best for: personal brands, LinkedIn B2B

#### 3.5 Weekly Class Funnel (Funil de Aula Semanal)
- Weekly content → email list → warming → session invitation
- Consistent pipeline, long-term play
- Best for: sustainable HT business

### Creative Types for Session Lead Gen:
- Video testimonials (highest conversion)
- Problem-agitation ads
- "Behind the scenes" of client results
- "Application closing in X days" (scarcity)
- Case study breakdowns

## 4. SHT (Semente High Ticket)

Adaptation of Lançamento Semente for high-ticket:
1. Build small audience (even 100-500 people)
2. Deliver free value (live workshop or webinar)
3. During the event, open applications for HT program
4. Conduct 1-on-1 sessions with applicants
5. Sell HT offer (R$3K-15K)

Key difference from regular Semente: instead of selling to the masses, you're identifying and qualifying the top 5-10% who are ready for HT.

## 5. AR (Acelerador de Resultados)

Scalable HT product (group, not 1-on-1):
- Group mentorship with fixed cohort
- Weekly group calls + individual check-ins
- Typically R$3K-8K per person
- 10-30 people per cohort
- 3-6 month duration
- Leverages your time: 1 hour serves 10-30 people vs 1

### AR Structure:
1. Onboarding call (individual)
2. Weekly group calls (60-90 min)
3. Monthly individual check-in (30 min)
4. Community/WhatsApp group for daily support
5. Templates, frameworks, swipe files
6. Graduation/certification at end

## 6. Delegação de Entrega (Delivery Delegation)

### When to delegate:
- When you're doing 10+ sessions/month
- When delivery quality drops due to volume
- When you want to scale beyond your time

### Mentor Training System:
1. **Selection**: Find people who've gotten results with your method
2. **Training**: 
   - Session script mastery
   - Objection handling
   - Diagnostic skills
   - Delivery standards
3. **Shadowing**: New mentor observes 5+ sessions
4. **Supervised**: New mentor runs sessions while you observe
5. **Independent**: Full delegation with quality tracking

### Tracking:
- Credit system per delivery
- Spreadsheet: Mentor / Client / Session Date / Satisfaction / Results
- Quality reviews monthly

## 7. Venda de Ingressos (Ticket Sales for Events)

### Multi-Lot Strategy:
- **Lote Zero (Lot 0)**: Pre-sale to existing audience at lowest price (creates urgency)
- **Lote 1**: Early bird price
- **Lote 2**: Regular price
- **Lote 3**: Last-minute price (highest)

### Revenue Stack for Live Events:
1. Ticket sales (front-end — funds marketing)
2. Order bump: event recording
3. Upsell at event: HT mentorship/program
4. Sponsorships (if applicable)
5. Networking/mastermind offer

## 10. KLT Framework (Know-Like-Trust)

### K — Know (Conhecer)
- **Objective**: Increase follower/awareness base
- **Structure**: Hook → Context/Proof → CTA (Follow)
- **Content**: Address problems (they may not even know they have)
- **Format**: Reels, short video

### L — Like (Gostar)
- **Structure**: Hook/Context → Content → CTA
- **Content**: Build affinity, show personality, values alignment

### T — Trust (Confiar)
- **Focus**: Product, Result, and Method
- **No CTA** — pure value delivery
- **3 Content types**:
  - C1: Result → Problem (show what's possible)
  - C2: Method → Solution (how you solve it)
  - C3: Product → Testimonials (social proof)

## 11. Mental Triggers for Sales (18 Triggers)

1. **Authority** — Credentials, positioning, visual authority
2. **Proof** — Testimonials (video > text > screenshot)
3. **Social Proof** — "If everyone is doing it..."
4. **Similarity** — "I was just like you..."
5. **Events** — Time-limited occurrences create urgency
6. **Anticipation** — Build excitement before the offer
7. **Story** — Narratives bypass sales resistance
8. **Reason/Why** — Explain the rationale
9. **Interaction** — Personalized engagement
10. **Scarcity** — Limited availability (must be real)
11. **Commitment/Coherence** — Small yeses lead to big yes
12. **Exclusivity** — Not for everyone
13. **Specificity** — 16,501 > "15,000+"
14. **Celebrity** — Be the interesting person in the room
15. **Curiosity** — Open loops that demand resolution
16. **Detachment** — Never appear desperate
17. **Choice Paradox** — Maximum 2 options when closing
18. **Integrity** — Complete transparency

**Rule**: All triggers must be truthful. Fabricated scarcity or fake proof destroys credibility.

## 12. Traffic Scale Framework

### Types:
- **Vertical Scale**: Increase budget on winning campaigns
- **Horizontal Scale**: Create new campaigns/audiences

### Structure:
- CBO (Campaign Budget Optimization): 5 ad sets, same audience, let Meta optimize
- ABO (Ad Set Budget Optimization): Individual budget per ad set, more control
- Advantage+: Broad targeting, let algorithm find best prospects

### Scale Rules:
- VMC (Average Customer Value) must be > CPA
- If VMC < CPA → cannot scale, fix funnel first
- Test 7 days ABO → graduate winners to CBO → scale
