## 1. Overview

The LCP (Lançamento com Custo Pago) is a 2-day intensive paid launch format. Unlike Tabari's Turbo (weekly/repeatable), Baldan's model is designed as a high-intensity, live 2-day event with structured pitch sequences.

### Key Differences from Tabari:
| Aspect | Tabari (Turbo) | Baldan (LCP) |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| Duration | 1 day (workshop) or 7 days (challenge) | 2 days intensive |
| Format | Can be recorded and repeated | Primarily live |
| Pitch timing | End of event | Progressive across 2 days |
| Scale model | Weekly replay | Periodic live events |
| Best for | Repeatable, automated | High-energy, live community |

## 2. Day 1 Structure (6 Sessions)

### Session 1.01 — Opening & Context
- Welcome and expectations setting
- "Why you're here" — connect to Roma
- Establish authority without bragging
- Community building moment (chat engagement)

### Session 1.02 — The Problem Deep Dive
- Articulate the problem better than they can
- Show the cost of inaction (Less Bad angle)
- Data and real examples
- "This is what's happening in your market right now"

### Session 1.03 — The Method Introduction
- Present your unique mechanism
- "This is HOW we solve it"
- Framework overview (3-5 steps)
- Proof that the method works (cases)

### Session 1.04 — Quick Win / Implementation
- Give attendees a tangible mini-result
- Exercise or template they can use immediately
- "You just did in 30 minutes what most people never figure out"
- This builds confidence and proves the method

### Session 1.05 — Case Studies & Social Proof
- Deep-dive into 3-5 real case studies
- Before/after with specific numbers
- Different profiles (so everyone sees themselves)
- "If they can do it, so can you"

### Session 1.06 — Day 1 Closing & Anticipation
- Recap what they learned
- "Tomorrow is where everything comes together"
- Homework/assignment to keep engagement
- Build anticipation for Day 2 (open loop)

## 3. Day 2 Structure (5 Sessions)

### Session 2.01 — Day 2 Opening & Recap
- Energy reset — "Welcome back"
- Recap Day 1 key insights
- Share wins from homework (social proof in real-time)
- Set frame for what's coming

### Session 2.02 — Advanced Content
- Deeper into the method
- "Here's what most people get wrong..."
- Advanced strategies that require guidance
- Position the gap between DIY and guided implementation

### Session 2.03 — The Transformation Story
- Full origin story (yours or client's)
- Emotional arc: struggle → discovery → transformation
- Connect to the Roma
- This is the "belief shift" moment

### Session 2.04 — The Offer
- Transition from content to offer
- Full offer presentation:
  - Name and positioning
  - What's included (deliverables stack)
  - Bonuses and their individual value
  - Guarantee (risk reversal)
  - Price (anchored against total value)
  - Payment options
- Q&A for objection handling

### Session 2.05 — Closing & Urgency
- Final testimonials (live if possible)
- Cart closing timeline
- "What happens if you don't act" (Less Bad)
- "What's possible when you do" (More Good)
- Final CTA with clear next steps
- Cart open window (typically 48-72 hours after event)

## 4. Conversion Planning

### Metrics to Track:
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Ticket purchases | Varies by price point |
| Show-up rate Day 1 | 60-74% |
| Retention Day 1→2 | 70%+ |
| Offer conversion | 5-15% of attendees |
| Revenue per attendee | Track and optimize |

### Price Planning:
- Calculate target revenue
- Work backwards to required attendance
- Factor in show-up rate for ticket sales target
- Example: Target R$100K → need 20 sales at R$5K → need 200 attendees at 10% conversion → need 300 tickets at 66% show-up
