# Fórmula de Lançamento (FL) — Complete Framework

Last updated: May 2026

> Source: Érico Rocha's Fórmula de Lançamento + FL Insider Implementation Days
> Consumed by: launch-strategist, cmo-strategist, funnel-architect, social-media-director

---

## 1. Three Pillars (Base Foundation)

The entire FL methodology rests on three pillars:

1. **Audiência (Audience)** — Build an engaged audience BEFORE launching. Without audience, nothing works.
2. **Lançamento (Launch)** — A structured event that converts audience into buyers through psychological triggers.
3. **Produto (Product)** — The offer itself. Must solve a real problem for a specific avatar.

**Priority order**: Audience → Launch → Product (you can launch before the product is fully built — Lançamento Semente proves this).

---

## 2. NAR Framework (Nicho-Avatar-Roma)

### Nicho (Niche)
- Define the specific market segment
- Must be narrow enough to be the "go-to expert" but wide enough for revenue

### Avatar
- **Demographics**: age, gender, location, income, profession
- **Psychographics**: fears, desires, frustrations, aspirations
- **DSA (Dores, Sonhos, Aspirações)**: the emotional core of all copy
- **Voice of Customer**: actual words they use (for copy matching)

### Roma (Destination)
- The attractive, simple destination the avatar wants to reach
- Must be articulated in one sentence
- Examples: "Faturar 6 em 7" (R$100K in 7 days), "Viver de internet", "Ter uma equipe que funciona sem você"
- Roma is NOT the product — it's the RESULT the product delivers
- Roma drives ALL communication: content, ads, emails, scripts

## 3. Launch Types

### 3.1 Lançamento Semente (Seed Launch)
**When**: First launch ever, validating product-market fit
**Duration**: ~4 weeks total
**Phases**:
1. **CLI (Conteúdo de Lançamento Interno)** — 14 days: Create warming content, build anticipation
2. **AI (Abertura de Inscrições)** — 3 days: Open registration for the live event
3. **Webinário** — 1 session: Deliver value + pitch (script: Roma → Proof → Story → Content → Offer → Ingredients)
4. **Carrinho (Cart)** — 5 days: Sales window open, recovery emails, urgency

**Target**: 500-1000 leads for first launch
**Key metric**: CPL formula = previous revenue / leads / 4

### 3.2 Lançamento Clássico (Classic Launch)
**When**: After successful Semente, for scaling
**Duration**: ~6-8 weeks total
**Phases**:
1. **CLI (Conteúdo de Lançamento Interno)** — Pre-launch content period
2. **AL (Antecipação do Lançamento)** — 14-28 days: Anticipation building, list warming
3. **PL (Pré-Lançamento)** — 7 days: 3 CPL videos (Content of Pre-Launch)
   - CPL1: Opportunity — show what's possible
   - CPL2: Transformation — prove it works (cases, method)
   - CPL3: Experience — let them taste it (mini-result)
4. **L (Lançamento)** — 7 days: Cart open, live event, urgency sequence

### 3.3 Ciclos Evolutivos (Evolutionary Cycles)
- After Semente → run 7 Classic Launches
- Spacing: 45-60 days between each launch
- Each cycle improves: better copy, more testimonials, refined offer, larger audience
- Milestones: 17 marcos from Semente to 6em7

## 4. Offer Construction (FL Method)

Every offer must have 6 components:

1. **Nome (Name)** — Specific, memorable, uses MAGIC formula (not generic)
2. **Roma** — The destination/result clearly articulated
3. **Entregáveis (Deliverables)** — What exactly the buyer receives (modules, hours, access)
4. **Bônus (Bonuses)** — Additional value items that reduce perceived risk
5. **Preço (Price)** — Positioned relative to the Roma value, not to cost
6. **Garantia (Guarantee)** — Risk reversal (7 days, 30 days, or results-based)

## 5. Árvore de Objeções (Objection Tree)

### Universal Objections (appear in every niche):
- **Tempo (Time)**: "I don't have time"
- **Dinheiro (Money)**: "I can't afford it"
- **Cônjuge (Spouse)**: "I need to talk to my partner"
- **Medo (Fear)**: "What if it doesn't work for me?"
- **Confiança (Trust)**: "I don't know if I can trust you"

### Specific Objections (niche-dependent):
- Map 5-10 objections specific to the product/avatar
- Each objection needs a "weakening strategy" embedded in content BEFORE the pitch

### Strategy:
- Objections must be weakened during CPL content, NOT during the pitch
- By the time the offer is presented, the avatar should have already overcome 80% of objections
- Use content, testimonials, and stories to address each one

## 6. Content System (Raiz + Nutella)

### Conteúdo Raiz (Root Content)
- **Duration**: 30+ minutes, deep-dive format
- **Purpose**: Establish authority, build trust, create retargeting audiences
- **5 Techniques for generating Raiz topics**:
  1. 3 areas of the Roma — break the destination into sub-topics
  2. Dores e Aspirações — address specific pains and desires
  3. Mitos — bust myths in the niche
  4. Atalhos e Obstáculos — shortcuts to avoid and obstacles to overcome
  5. Estudos de Caso — real case studies with results
- **Roteiro Raiz**: Connect the "how" with the Roma

### Conteúdo Nutella (Short-form Content)
- Derived FROM Raiz — cut, repurpose, adapt
- Short-form: Reels, Shorts, TikTok
- Purpose: Reach new audiences, drive to Raiz

### Linhas Editoriais (Editorial Lines):
1. **Mão na massa** — How-to, practical content
2. **Bastidores** — Behind the scenes
3. **Consultoria** — Advisory/diagnostic style
4. **Entrevistas** — Conversations with relevant people
5. **Podcast** — Long-form conversational

### Distribution:
1. Allocate budget for distribution
2. Publish on all platforms
3. Boost/distribute with paid traffic
4. Collect data (views, engagement, retargeting pixels)

## 7. Método (Method Formalization)

Every expert must formalize their method:
- 3-5 main steps
- Each step has sub-steps
- Named with a memorable framework name
- Visualized as a journey/roadmap
- This becomes the backbone of CPL content and the product structure

---

## 8. Webinar/Event Script Structure

The proven script for the live selling event:

1. **Roma** — Paint the destination vividly
2. **Prova (Proof)** — Show it's achievable (testimonials, data)
3. **História (Story)** — Your origin story connecting to the Roma
4. **Conteúdo (Content)** — Deliver genuine value (method overview)
5. **Oferta (Offer)** — Present the complete offer with all 6 components
6. **Ingredientes (Ingredients)** — Break down what's included, stack value

**Key rule**: 80% content / 20% pitch. The content itself should make the offer feel inevitable.

## 10. SNA (Sistema de Navegação do Aluno)

Operational checklist system from FL Insider, organized by phase:

### Phase 1 — Preparation
- Define NAR (Nicho, Avatar, Roma)
- Define Offer and Method
- Create social media presence
- Establish content frequency
- Plan creatives
- Build capture and thank-you pages
- Create capture campaign

### Phase 2 — Lead Capture
- Activate lead capture campaigns
- Warm subscribers with content
- Prepare webinar script
- Create product on platform (Hotmart/Eduzz)

### Phase 3 — Launch Execution
- Execute webinar/event
- Open cart
- Recovery emails and messages
- Manual sales recovery

### Phase 4 — Post-Launch
- Close cart
- Inaugural class
- Fill debriefing document
- Post debriefing for community analysis
- Partnership evaluation meeting

---

## 11. Método LEOA

Sequential priority framework:
1. **L — Lançamento**: Get the launch mechanics right
2. **E — Entrega**: Deliver an excellent product experience
3. **O — Oferta**: Optimize the offer based on feedback
4. **A — Audiência**: Scale audience building

Order matters: don't try to scale audience before you've proven launch + delivery + offer work.
