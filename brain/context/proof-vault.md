## How to Read This Document

Each proof entry is tokenized for AI agents to select the right proof for the right context. Use the `use-in` field to match proof to channel. Use the `pillar` field to match proof to offer. Fields marked `[TODO: Moroni adicionar]` require founder input before use in copy or ads.

## Selection Logic for AI Agents

- Landing pages: prefer `case-study` type with specific revenue or time metrics
- Ad creatives: prefer `metric` or short `testimonial-video` with one sharp number
- Proposals: prefer `case-study` type matching the prospect's industry
- Social content: prefer `testimonial-text` or `metric` for carousels and LinkedIn
- Email sequences: prefer `case-study` for nurture, `testimonial-text` for objection handling
