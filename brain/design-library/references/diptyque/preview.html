08 / Dark Section — Product Glow

The Candle Glow Effect

On dark backgrounds, products are presented with a warm amber radial gradient glow. The flame flickers subtly. This is Diptyque's signature digital expression of candlelight.

09 / Accent Section — Diptyque Green

The Intimate Room

In Diptyque's physical stores, the scheme gets progressively darker and more intimate, culminating in green-lacquered rooms for personalized services. This dark green (#2D4A3E) translates to accent sections for premium, personalized, or editorial content.
