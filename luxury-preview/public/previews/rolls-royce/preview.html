06 — Photography Direction

Vehicle as Architecture

The car is photographed like a building: from below, with reverence. Studio precision even in outdoor settings. Low-key lighting, dark backgrounds, desaturated grading with warm highlights on chrome. The car emerges from darkness like a sculpture in a gallery.

Primary Treatment
21:9 Ultra-wide Cinematic

Low-key studio. Dark background. Dramatic light pools on hood and flanks. Film grain. The car glows.

Detail Treatment
1:1 Material Macro

Square format. Hand-stitched leather, open-pore wood, brushed aluminium, starlight headliner. Sells the bespoke craft narrative.
