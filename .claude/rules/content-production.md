# Content Production Rules — REIS IA

> Used by: social-media-director, hook-specialist, reels-scriptwriter, linkedin-strategist, carousel-designer-writer
> Purpose: Standard content production framework based on FL Raiz/Nutella system + Insider methodology

---

## 1. Content Hierarchy: Raiz → Nutella

### Raiz (Root Content)
- **Duration**: 30+ minutes minimum
- **Purpose**: Establish authority, build deep trust, create retargeting audiences
- **Format**: Long-form video (YouTube, Lives, IGTV), podcast episodes
- **Frequency**: 1-2 per week during active periods

### Nutella (Short-form Content)
- **MUST be derived from Raiz** — never create Nutella from scratch
- **Purpose**: Reach new audiences, drive to Raiz content
- **Format**: Reels, Shorts, TikTok, carousel summaries
- **Frequency**: 3-5 per week (derived from each Raiz)

### Rule: Raiz ALWAYS comes first. Nutella is a derivative, not an original.

---

## 2. Raiz Topic Generation (5 Techniques)

Every Raiz topic must come from one of these 5 sources:

1. **3 Areas of the Roma** — Break the destination into sub-topics
2. **Dores e Aspiracoes** — Address specific pains and desires of the avatar
3. **Mitos** — Bust myths in the niche
4. **Atalhos e Obstaculos** — Shortcuts and common obstacles
5. **Estudos de Caso** — Real case studies with specific results

## 3. Editorial Lines

Content must rotate across these editorial types:

| Line | What | Frequency |
|------|------|-----------|
| **Mao na massa** | How-to, practical, step-by-step | 2x/week |
| **Bastidores** | Behind the scenes, process | 1x/week |
| **Consultoria** | Advisory, diagnostic, "if I were you" | 1x/week |
| **Entrevistas** | Conversations with relevant people | 1x/2 weeks |
| **Podcast** | Long-form conversational | 1x/week |

## 4. Content-to-Launch Pipeline

Content strategy must align with the launch phase:

| Phase | Content Type | Objective |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| Pre-launch (ongoing) | Raiz + Nutella | Build audience, establish authority |
| CLI (Conteudo de Lancamento Interno) | Warming content | Anticipation, problem awareness |
| AL (Antecipacao) | Proof + stories | Build desire, weaken objections |
| PL (Pre-Lancamento) | CPL 1-3 | Opportunity → Transformation → Experience |
| L (Lancamento) | Event + urgency | Convert |
| Post-launch | Debrief + new Raiz | Reset cycle |

## 5. Distribution Rules

1. Every Raiz MUST be distributed with paid traffic (even small budget)
2. Nutella serves organic + paid amplification
3. Track all content with UTM parameters
4. Collect data: views, engagement time, retargeting pixel fires
5. Content performance feeds back into topic selection

## 6. KLT Framework Integration

All content must map to the KLT (Know-Like-Trust) funnel:

- **K (Know)**: Hook → Context/Proof → CTA (Follow). Addresses problems. Format: Reels.
- **L (Like)**: Hook/Context → Content → CTA. Builds affinity and personality.
- **T (Trust)**: Product, Result, Method. NO CTA — pure value.
  - C1: Result → Problem (what's possible)
  - C2: Method → Solution (how to solve)
  - C3: Product → Testimonials (social proof)

## 7. Hormozi Angle Integration

Every piece of content MUST leverage at least one Hormozi angle:
- **More Good**: What they gain
- **Less Bad**: What they avoid
- **More Good for Others**: Status/reputation gain
- **Less Bad for Others**: Risk elimination

## 8. Quality Checklist

Before publishing any content:
- [ ] Derived from Raiz (if Nutella) or from the 5 techniques (if Raiz)
- [ ] Maps to KLT stage
- [ ] Uses at least 1 Hormozi angle
- [ ] Fits an editorial line
- [ ] Has clear CTA appropriate to the KLT stage
- [ ] Went through humanizer if written by AI
- [ ] Portuguese accents correct
