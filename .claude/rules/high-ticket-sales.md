# High-Ticket Sales Rules — REIS IA

> Used by: offer-architect, funnel-architect, direct-response-copywriter, cmo-strategist
> Purpose: Operational rules for high-ticket sales processes managed through the agent system

---

## 1. Session Protocol

### Pre-Session (Mandatory):
- Lead must have completed an application/qualification form
- AI pre-session report generated (DISC profile, pain map, consciousness level)
- Closer must review report BEFORE the session
- Never enter a session cold

### During Session:
- Follow the 6-phase script (Rapport → Diagnosis → Vision → Offer → Objections → Close)
- Never pitch before Phase 3 (Vision)
- Use the lead's OWN WORDS when presenting the solution
- Maximum 2 options when closing (Choice Paradox)
- Record every session (with permission) for debrief

### Post-Session (Mandatory):
- AI debrief generated from recording/transcription
- Log: Date / Closer / Lead / DISC / Objections / Outcome / Revenue
- Follow-up sequence activated for non-buyers (12 messages, 10 days)

## 2. Offer Rules for HT

- Every HT offer must follow Hormozi Grand Slam format
- Value stack BEFORE price reveal — always
- Guarantee is mandatory (minimum 7-day risk reversal)
- Price must be anchored against the Roma value, not against competitors
- Never discount — add value instead

## 3. Funnel Selection for HT

Match funnel to business maturity:

| Stage | Recommended Funnel | Why |
|-------|-------------------|-----|
| Starting (0-10 clients) | Social Selling + Free Bait | Low cost, build proof |
| Growing (10-50 clients) | Application + Paid Bait | Higher quality leads |
| Scaling (50+ clients) | Weekly Class + Application | Consistent pipeline |

## 4. Mental Triggers in Copy

All HT copy must use these triggers strategically:
- **Authority** — in every piece
- **Proof** — video testimonials preferred
- **Specificity** — exact numbers always (R$2.3M not "millions")
- **Scarcity** — must be REAL (limited spots, cohort dates)
- **Detachment** — never sound desperate

## 5. Follow-up Rules

- Maximum 12 touches over 10 days
- Alternate between substantive messages and light touches
- Include social proof in touch #4
- Include exclusivity in touch #8
- Final touch is "last call" — then stop
- Never be pushy — be professionally persistent

## 6. KPI Targets

| Metric | Minimum | Good | Excellent |
|--------|---------|------|-----------|
| Application-to-session rate | 30% | 50% | 70% |
| Session show-up rate | 60% | 75% | 85% |
| Close rate | 15% | 25% | 40%+ |
| Average ticket | R$3K | R$5K | R$10K+ |
| Client lifetime value | 1.5x ticket | 2x ticket | 3x+ ticket |

## 7. Delegation Rules

- HT sales sessions require trained closers only
- New closers must shadow 5+ sessions before going solo
- Quality tracking mandatory (wins/errors/objections per session)
- Monthly quality review of recorded sessions
- Close rate below 15% → retraining required
