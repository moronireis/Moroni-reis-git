## Advisory Pricing Reality (u4digital model, May 2026)

- Setup/pilot price: R$5K (a vista R$4.5-5K, parcelado 3x ~R$1.85K)
- Anchor price: R$8K (reference for value perception + planned escalation)
- Post-project recurrence: R$497/mo (maintenance) or R$1.5K/mo (continuous advisory)
- Consistent with RHF Talentos pilot: R$5K setup + R$497/mo recurrence
- Natori Foods reference: R$10K (more complex scope)

**Why:** These prices were validated through actual proposals (RHF closed at R$5K, Natori at R$10K). The R$5K entry creates a no-brainer for experts spending R$12-25K/mo on operations.

**How to apply:** For future advisory offers targeting digital experts/infoprodutores in Brazil, start at R$5K setup for first 10-15 clients, then escalate to R$8-12K as case portfolio grows. Never go below R$3K — filters out unqualified buyers.
