## 8. Design Direction Summary for Agents

### Aesthetic DNA
- **Core:** Luxury femininity meets empowerment — not delicate/fragile, but powerful/regal
- **References:** Dior, Bvlgari, Gucci (luxury fashion houses as visual language)
- **Mood:** "My brand is a reflection of my essence: timeless, sophisticated"
- **Contrast:** Classical elements (statues, shields, mirrors) + modern urban confidence

### Key Design Principles
1. **Regal, not cute** — crowns, shields, mirrors as power symbols
2. **Blue + Gold + White** as primary palette (with emerald for Academy)
3. **Script + Serif typography** for luxury feel
4. **Dark backgrounds** for premium impact (creatives), clean white for editorial
5. **Botanical accents** — orchids, florals as elegance markers (not decoration)
6. **Porcelain/Chinese blue-white** as a recurring pattern reference
7. **Double exposure** as an art technique for social/brand content

### Anti-Patterns (Avoid)
- Cheap/generic stock photography
- Neon or overly saturated colors
- Casual/playful typography
- Tech/modern minimalism (this is luxury fashion, not SaaS)
- Excessive emojis or informal design elements
