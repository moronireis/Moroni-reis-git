Concept: Identical to 1A but with the thin accent line removed. The composition ends with EVENTOS text, and the teal bars above are the only colored elements. Concept: A teal dot (3.5px radius) is placed between the G and J, reading as "G . J" -- a visual interpunct that becomes a brand element. The dot sits at the vertical center of the cap-height (y=55), creating a bridge between the two initials. No accent line below, so the dot is the only "extra" element beyond bars and letters. The dot echoes the round-capped bars, maintaining the geometric language. At small sizes, the dot reads as a period-like separator; at large sizes, it becomes a distinctive brand detail. Best for: Creating a unique punctuation mark that can be reused across the brand (e.g., "G.J" in text, the dot as a standalone favicon mark).

Concept: A vertical/stacked layout where the teal bars sit above the GJ letterforms like an architectural canopy or crown element.
